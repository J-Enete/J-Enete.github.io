UX Case Study: Lagos Smart Meter Dashboard

A power monitoring and management system for admins and consumers in Lagos, Nigeria.

  • Platform: Desktop and Mobile (client/app)
  • Client: Lagos State Electricity Board (LSEB)
  • Industry: Power
  • Live: No
  • Design team: 1 persons
  • My role: Lead designer

Project Overview

The Lagos Smart Meter Dashboard is a comprehensive system designed to revolutionize power supply management for administrators and enhance the experience for consumers in the Lagos power grid. The aim is to provide real-time insights, improve operational efficiency, and empower users with greater control over their power consumption.

Project Goals

  1. Streamline Power Supply Management.
  2. Enhance User Experience for Administrators and Customers.
  3. Provide Real-time Monitoring and Insights.

Research Phase

Conducted interviews and surveys to understand the pain points of administrators and customers. Discovered challenges in managing power distribution efficiently and issues related to billing transparency.

Design and Prototyping

  1. User-Friendly Interface: Created an intuitive dashboard with a clean and user-friendly design, ensuring easy navigation for administrators.
  2. Real-time Monitoring: Implemented live monitoring features to enable administrators to track power consumption, identify outages, and manage resources effectively.
  3. Customer Empowerment: Developed a customer portal allowing users to monitor their energy consumption, view billing details, and receive real-time alerts.

Development Phase

  1. Responsive Design: Ensured the dashboard is accessible across various devices, providing a seamless experience for administrators on desktops and customers on mobile devices.
  2. Integration of Smart Meters: Implemented a connection with smart meters to gather real-time data on power consumption and facilitate automated billing processes.

Testing and Iteration

Conducted usability tests with administrators and customers to gather feedback. Made iterative improvements based on user input, focusing on enhancing data visualization and optimizing user workflows.

Launch and User Training

Launched the Lagos Smart Meter Dashboard and conducted training sessions for administrators and customers to ensure effective utilization.

Results

  1. Improved Operational Efficiency: Administrators can now monitor power supply in real-time, identify issues promptly, and allocate resources efficiently.
  2. Enhanced Customer Experience: Customers have greater transparency into their power usage, leading to better energy management and increased satisfaction.
  3. Reduction in Operational Costs: The automated processes and real-time monitoring have resulted in cost savings for the power distribution system.

Conclusion

The Lagos Smart Meter Dashboard has successfully transformed power supply management in Lagos, providing a powerful tool for administrators and empowering customers with greater control over their energy consumption. The iterative design process and user-centered approach have led to a solution that meets the diverse needs of all stakeholders in the Lagos power grid.
